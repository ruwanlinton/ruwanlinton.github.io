About Ruwan

Ruwan is the Chief Technology Officer of AdroitLogic. He is passionate about software design and architecture.

He is also an elected member of the Apache Software Foundation, where he once led the Apache Synapse project, while contributing to Apache WS and Axis2 projects.

Ruwan graduated from the University of Moratuwa specialized in Computer Science and Engineering with a first class honours, and has presented and published papers at leading conferences. His key competency is in Enterprise Integration Solutions/Patterns, Distributed Computing and Programing Languages. Ruwan is an innovative software designer and an architect with excellent technology skills such as SOA, EAI and Cloud Computing.

Ruwan's role in AdroitLogic spans across providing technical vision to product/customer deliveries. Ruwan was a core technical team member of the WSO2 ESB team since 2006 before joining AdroitLogic in 2011.

He is crazy about cars, loves to drive fast, enjoys music, watching movies and travelling around the world.
